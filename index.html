I am a PhD student in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Scientific Computing at Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee. My research interests include nonlinear partial differential equations, conservation laws, Lie symmetry analysis, invariant and exact solutions, numerical simulation of PDEs and physics-informed neural networks.

Research Interests

  • Lie symmetry analysis and invariant solutions
  • Conservation laws
  • Exact and solitonic solutions
  • Numerical simulation of PDEs
  • Physics-informed neural networks
