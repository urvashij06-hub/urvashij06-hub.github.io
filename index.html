Urvashi Joshi

PhD Student · Applied Mathematics & Scientific Computing

I am a PhD student in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Scientific Computing at the Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee. My research focuses on nonlinear partial differential equations, conservation laws, Lie symmetry analysis, exact and invariant solutions, and numerical and data-driven methods for complex physical and biological systems. I am also interested in physics-informed neural networks for PDE-based models.

Research Interests

  • Nonlinear partial differential equations
  • Lie symmetry analysis and invariant solutions
  • Conservation laws
  • Exact and solitonic solutions
  • Numerical simulation of PDEs
  • Physics-informed neural networks
