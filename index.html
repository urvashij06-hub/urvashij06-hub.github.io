Urvashi Joshi

PhD Student · Applied Mathematics & Scientific Computing

I am a PhD student at the Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee. My research focuses on nonlinear partial differential equations, conservation laws, Lie symmetry analysis, exact and invariant solutions, and numerical and data-driven methods for complex physical and biological systems. I am also interested in physics-informed neural networks for PDE-based models.
